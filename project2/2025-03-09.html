Overview

BoodaTrack is a software application that utilizes AI to analyze a player's badminton play

The app specializes in analyzing footwork specifically and using AI to derive specific insights like flagging play that increases chance of injury

The app is structured as a team platform where team users can log in, upload footage, and have it analyzed and sorted by tournament, player, etc.

Current Development

This is a project I started in January 2025 and have been working on in my freetime

It includes features like injury prevention and player tracking over time
